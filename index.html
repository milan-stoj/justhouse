About

JustHouseMKE is a non profit organization based in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. Our mission is to create a Tenant and Landlord Bill of Rights for the citizens of Milwaukee. Founded by a group of concerned citizens, JustHouseMKE is driven by a passion for justice and a desire to make a positive impact in our community. We are committed to working with lawmakers, landlords, and renters to create a more fair and equitable rental environment for all.

Our Approach

JustHouse works to achieve justice in housing in Milwaukee through policy, advocacy, and organizing.

Our Mission

We believe that all renters deserve to live in safe and habitable housing, and we are dedicated to advocating for the rights of tenants and helping to improve the rental market in Milwaukee.

Kat Klawes

  • Housing Policy Advocate
    She, Her, Hers

Kat Klawes (she/her) is an educator and activist. She was born in Milwaukee and has worked in both Michigan and Wisconsin. Her work includes being a bill author, and running advocacy groups. Housing is a personal issue for Kat as she experienced homelessness at age 4 when her family home had a fire, causing her and her family to have to move into a one-room motel. Kat lives on the East Side of Milwaukee with her partner and three cats. Her work has been featured in the Huffington Post, NY Mag, and has received awards from Buzzfeed and Bank of America.
